# MiroFish Discovery Diverse Frontier Fast Path Note

## Why

The discovery process had become too slow because it drifted into hand-curating small groups of candidate chips before the intake surface was fully used.

The better operating rule is:

- maximize diversity at intake
- let the agent swarm produce the raw frontier
- canonicalize the frontier
- defer simulation until after the broad intake exists

## What Changed

- Added a `diverse_frontier` scaffold profile that scales cleanly beyond the `100`-agent viral pilot.
- Generated a full `1000`-agent diverse frontier scaffold and materialized cluster directory.
- Kept the profile broad instead of over-optimizing around a few favorite wedges.
