# Spark Domain Chip Labs

**Type**: Meta-research chip and domain chip R&D lab
**Domain**: chip-research
**Version**: 0.1.0
**Status**: Active

## What This Is

The research lab for the Spark domain chip ecosystem. A meta-chip that evaluates other chips, improves chip-building methodology, discovers new domain opportunities, and studies how domain specialization contributes to recursive self-improvement and collective intelligence.

## Connected Systems

- **spark-researcher**: Autoloop runtime (the lab runs as a chip within spark-researcher)
- **spark-swarm**: Collective intelligence platform (the lab emits insights, masteries, contradictions)
- **Domain chips**: The lab evaluates and improves the entire chip portfolio

## Agent Research Team

Seven specialized workstreams within one governing flywheel:

1. **Frontier Scout** -- discovers new domain opportunities
2. **Methodology Researcher** -- improves chip-building methodology
3. **Chip Architect** -- designs new chip manifests and graduation paths
4. **Benchmark Engineer** -- creates and maintains quality benchmarks
5. **Integration Specialist** -- ensures spark-researcher/swarm compatibility
6. **Growth Analyst** -- tracks ecosystem adoption and prioritizes domains
7. **AGI Theorist** -- researches recursive self-improvement and collective intelligence

## Key Principle

The lab is itself a chip. It follows the same spark-chip.v1 contract as every other domain chip.
This means the lab can be evaluated, improved, and graduated using its own methodology.
